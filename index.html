The Rebound Solution

One workflow. Full recovery.

A single integrated system — intake to resale — that eliminates delays, leakage, and unnecessary write-offs.

01

Returns Operations

Full ownership of intake, sorting, and grading. Every unit assessed and logged with zero leakage. A single clean workflow replaces fragmented handoffs.

02

Precision Refurbishment

Brand-specific SOPs for every material — leather, mesh, rubber, suede. Multi-step revival: surface treatment, odour neutralisation, sole restoration, detailing, and QC. Like-new output, every time.

03

Graded Resale & Recycling

Units graded as Like New, Refurbished, or Recycle — and routed accordingly. Brand-compliant resale through authorised channels. Non-salvageable units disposed per SWM regulations.

Liquidation Partnership

A smarter exit for
your returns.

Instead of bulk liquidation at floor price, Rebound offers a structured resale partnership — upfront recovery plus ongoing revenue share on every batch.

How the partnership works

Structured supply + revenue share model

  • Rebound buys returned/unsold footwear at a fixed lot rate of ₹250/kg — simple, weight-based pricing with no per-pair complexity.
  • We take full ownership of refurbishment, logistics, warehousing, resale, and customer service — zero operational lift on your end.
  • You receive 10% of Rebound's gross profit per batch as ongoing revenue share — paid within 30 days of batch close.
  • Full transparency: per-batch unit economics shared on buy cost, refurb cost, resale price, saleable %, and profit.
  • Starts at a 500-pair pilot. Success triggers a rolling monthly volume commitment.
