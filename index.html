Liquidation Partnership

A structured resale
partnership model.

We handle the entire journey — from acquiring your non-saleable stock to finding the right buyer — so you get value back with full visibility and zero operational burden.

Step 01 — Source

We acquire your non-saleable stock

Rebound purchases returned, unsold, or non-saleable footwear and apparel directly from you at a mutually agreed per-kg rate. Simple, weight-based pricing — no complexity, no ambiguity.

Step 02 — Refurbish

In-house refurbishment — fully our headache

Every unit goes through our proprietary refurbishment process in our facility. Cleaning, restoration, detailing, quality grading — all handled end-to-end by Rebound. You don't need to lift a finger.

Brand-compliant process · Zero informal exposure
Step 03 — Report

Full visibility — batch reports shared with you

After each batch, you receive a complete report: units received, condition grading, refurbishment outcomes, saleable count, and units recycled or disposed. Complete transparency, every cycle.

Per-batch reporting · Graded unit breakdown · Disposal compliance
Step 04 — Sell

We find the right buyer, through the right channel

Rebound identifies and manages authorised resale channels for refurbished stock — ensuring products reach genuine buyers at fair value, keeping your brand protected from grey markets.

Step 05 — Share

Profit shared back to you — every batch

Once a batch closes, a pre-agreed share of the gross profit is transferred back to you. You earn from stock that would otherwise be a write-off — with no effort and no risk on your side.

Agreed profit share · Paid per batch close · Fully documented
